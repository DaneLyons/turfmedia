Relative Passing Yards

All QB passing yards are plotted in relation to the selected QB (currently {{players[pacePlayer].name}}). That's why the selected player's line appears as a flat line in the middle of the graph. This makes it easier to compare players.

Try selecting Peyton Manning and highlighting Drew Brees. Manning was very consistent and outplayed Brees early in his career. Then Brees got traded to the Saints. His trajectory dramatically changed and he clearly outplayed Manning throughout the middle of his career. At this pace, Drew Brees should hold the record for passing yards in about a year.
